Top 5 Teams Entering the 2025 Season

  1. San Francisco 49ers — The most balanced team in football and ready for another Super Bowl run.
  2. Houston Texans — C.J. Stroud is ascending and now has elite help with Diggs on the roster.
  3. New York Jets — If Rodgers stays healthy, their offense plus elite defense is scary.
  4. Kansas City Chiefs — Never count them out with Mahomes under center.
  5. Detroit Lions — Great coaching, discipline, and young talent. Detroit is here to stay.
