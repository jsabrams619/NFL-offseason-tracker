Blockbuster Offseason Moves

  • Aaron Rodgers signs with Pittsburgh Steelers – One-year, $13.65M deal to play and mentor rookie QB Will Howard.
  • DK Metcalf traded to the Pittsburgh Steelers – Seahawks send a 2nd-round pick (#52) and 6th-round pick (#185) to Steelers for a 7th-round pick (#223). DK signs a 5-year, $150M extension ($30M/year).
  • Raiders trade for Geno Smith – Acquired for a third-round pick, signed a two-year, $85.5M deal. Tom Brady opposed signing Sam Darnold.
  • Stefon Diggs signs with Patriots – Veteran WR inks a three-year, $69M max deal following an injury-riddled season in Houston.
  • Sam Darnold lands in Seattle – After a breakout year, Darnold signs a three-year, $100.5M contract with the Seahawks.
  • Russell Wilson signs with Giants – Veteran QB joins on a one-year, $10.5M deal to compete for starting role.
  • Davante Adams signs with Rams – Two-year, $46M deal including $26M guaranteed.
  • Brock Purdy extension with 49ers – Five-year, $265M contract with team support.
  • Justin Fields signs with Jets – Two-year, $40M deal with a rising Jets roster.
  • Daniel Jones joins Colts – Signs a one-year, $14M contract to reset career.
